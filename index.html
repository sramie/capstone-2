About Me

I began my journey into quality while working for Rubbermaid (TN) back in 2011. I knew I wanted to make a difference when it came to customer satisfaction. I acquired a lot of knowledge regarding how high quaility items are handled and assisted in perfecting many of the procedures during my stay.
After moving to Kentucky, I joined a different field and began the education of electronic components. I was able to study under a great mentor and continue my interest in providing quality products to our clients. I never shy away from new challenges and I am always on the lookout for new possibilites.
I've recently moved to Germany where I've rediscovered my interest in coding. I remember learning Html and CSS back in the MySpace era and wanted to delve more into it again. I found the Udemy classes and have been throwing myself back into the mix to once again get a feel for it. I love designing the pages!

All Work and No Play

When I decided to move to Germany I made sure to bring my boy with me, a Siberian Husky named Baldr. He's been the star of several of my projects thus far and I can assure you he's super pleased with the attention.
Aside from serving his royal Husky-ness I find myself dabbling quite recently into 3D printing. I've been learning a few programs, Lychee, 3Dbuilder, and Chitubox, because I have an end goal of actually creating my own 3D model blueprints.
I'm also an avid PC gamer, have been since I was a child, and I enjoy taking part in Alpha/Beta testing. I think this goes back to my quality minded thinking and I enjoy knowing I can help make a better user experience for everyone. I would love to eventually go into quality testing as a career.
